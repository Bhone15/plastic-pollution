Our volunteers
need your support

    Trash Hero mobilises thousands of people around the world to take action on waste in their communities, change their everyday behaviour and reduce their dependency on single-use plastic. We rely on your help to do this. Make an online donation or a monthly gift to Trash Hero now. Together we make a difference!

Where does your money go?

100% of your donation (after the payment fees*) will go toward our programmes.
For Example:

  • Volunteers who run weekly cleanups locally can receive training, t-shirts and marketing materials.
  • We can print free materials for our hands-on kids’ education programme, again staffed by volunteers.
  • We can provide stock and transport costs for our community bottle refill programme that saves millions of plastic water bottles from being used and discarded.
  • We can support environmental workshops on improving waste management, and zero waste initiatives.

All of these things are made possible by donors like you. Giving regularly is one of the best ways to donate and make a contribution to a cleaner ocean and planet. Every amount, no matter how small, counts. Thank you!
